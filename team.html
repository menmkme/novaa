PROF. DOYIN SALAMI

Prof Doyin Salami(Nigeria) Dr. Doyin Salami is a full-time member of the faculty at the Lagos Business School (LBS), Pan-Atlantic University where he is an Associate Professor. In addition to teaching, he works as the CEO of Kainos Edge Consulting Limited. He is also the principal consultant for Edward Kingston Associates.His consulting activities have included assignments for the Department for International Development (DFID), World Bank, United Nations Industrial Development Organisation (UNIDO), and United States Agency for International Development (USAID). Dr. Salami is a past member of the Federal Government's Economic Management Team He also served as a member of the Monetary Policy Committee of the Central Bank of Nigeria until recently. He serves as a Board Member of the Nigerian Economic Summit Group, and is the Executive Director for the African Business Research Ltd, United Kingdom. Dr. Salami is an accomplished economist and has a doctorate degree from Queen Mary College, University of London.

BARR. CHINENYE UWANAKA

Chinenye is a Lawyer and Social Entrepreneur who is passionate about Nation building and fighting extreme poverty and Injustice in Africa. TFA provides services to clients in the private sector, non-profit and public sector. DR. UMMA YAHAYA

Dr. Umma is a proficient medical doctor with specialization in the field of obstetrics and gynecology. She holds a Master of Science in Public Health and a Diploma in Reproductive and Sexual Health, both earned from the prestigious London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine. Dr. Umma's primary focus lies in the realm of women's health, and she has a keen interest in public policy and initiatives aimed at promoting the well-being and advancement of women. With her extensive training and experience as a gynecologist, her expertise encompasses a wide array of areas, including women's and gender-related concerns, addressing disparities, healthrelated matters, and population-based projections, all underscored by her fervent commitment to meticulous planning. Driven by an unwavering determination to excel and contribute positively to the global landscape of women's health, Dr. Umma is dedicated to rectifying the inequities in service delivery to those in need.
